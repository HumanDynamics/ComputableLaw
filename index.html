About the Book

The Computable Law eBook is comprised of two volumes: Volume 1: Will highlight Legal Hacking as a means "to use use law to improve the nature and speed of technoloical innovation or to use innovation and technology to improve the law and legal process." Volume 2: Will detail technical topics related to the law such as data science, systems design, networks, eContracts/eSignatures, digital statutes, etc.

Book Design

The open source Computable Law eBook is designed to be a collaborative series that engages readers/users through interactive team exercices, project templates, problem sets as well as an updatable site for user and expanded versions of the book.
